New Denim for Myntra

Myntra is a mass-market fashion aggregator, stocking the latest and broadest of trends across a variety of brands, including their own lines. For the Kyoorius Young Blood Award 2024, they put up a brief asking entrants to 'Reimagine Denim for India'.

So my art partner and me assembled a team, designed a modular denim kurta, prototyped it, branded it, and well, won the Red Elephant.

Long Copy for The BMW X7 (Pitch-work)

BMW India put up a closed pitch asking a few agencies for help with solving a significant business problem. The X7 wasn't selling enough. And adding salt to the wound, the GLS was doing quite well.

So a repositioning for the product was asked. And this long copy ad is one of the creations I wanted to pitch with. While it didn't make the pitch deck, it did make my portfolio. And no, my agency didn't win the business.

Speculative Performance Brand Campaign for Hermès

Sometime in January 2025, I wrote on LinkedIn about how any brand can use almost any marketing channel, provided they're creatively strategic about it.

An example I cited was how Hermès, the haute couture fashion house could run performance ads. To reinforce the brand. A smart, seasoned commenter asked to see a sample. So I made these, inspired by the house's Footsteps Across The World Collection.

Branding and Launch for Runwal Central Park

Runwal Enterprises decided to build-out, and sell the last remaining phase of Runwal City Centre in Kanjurmarg, and approached our agency for the branding and launch.

They came with the name Central Park, since the towers would run right alongside a huge park. With all this in place, I went ahead and cracked the positioning line 'Where Life Loves You.' Then, my art director worked her magic.

Digital Film for Metro Shoes:
Stay-at-home vs Working Mom

This is the first episode of a digital film campaign for Metro Shoes. The idea was to create 4 films that address common societal snap judgements, pushing viewers to think twice before judging, and lead with empathy and kindness instead. Enter: Walk a Mile

I worked on writing the films, helped shoot them, assisted with post-production, and exercised overall creative control. I also realized that I really love being on set.
